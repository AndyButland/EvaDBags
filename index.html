Our Story

Born in Italy from a love of natural materials and mindful design, Eva D creates handcrafted textile bags that blend Mediterranean warmth with timeless elegance. Each piece is made by hand using eco-friendly fabrics, bamboo handles, and gold-tone accents — because true luxury lies in what is thoughtfully made.

Sustainably Crafted

We choose eco-friendly textiles and natural materials, crafting each bag with respect for the environment and the hands that make them.

Uniquely Yours

Every bag is one of a kind, handmade with artisanal care. No two pieces are exactly alike — each carries the character of its maker.

Timeless Design

Classic lines, natural textures, and refined details create a style that transcends seasons and trends.
